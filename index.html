About Me

I Robin Collins in High Halstow a small village on the Hoo Peninsular, several miles from the once City of Rochester.
I have been taking photographs professionally for over 25 years and embarked in website design when I didn't want to pay someone else what was extremely expensive in early 2005, it hasn't changed much in 2020 but the technologies are amazing and will boost a company's brand.
I used to use Serif and Adobe Muse including templates on privately owned servers to speed up my efficiency, however it became apparent width the support for the software becoming obsolete and a decline in my clients I went back to the drawing board and embarked on a course as a full stack developer with Code Institute where I should be fully qualified high end Full Stack Developer. I will be efficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python and more.
In my spare time I like to visit the gym to keep me fit enough to take part in obstacle course racing. To wind-down on a Sunday I endulge in some Archery and in the winter months I have the guilty sin of still online gaming.
